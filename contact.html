Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

We typically respond to service requests within 24 hours. For emergency services, we aim to respond within 2 hours.

Do you offer recycling services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive recycling services for various materials including paper, plastic, glass, and metal. We're committed to sustainable waste management.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all parishes across Jamaica, with our main operations based in Kingston. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific area.

How do I pay for services?

We accept various payment methods including cash, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. Payment details will be provided with your service quote.
